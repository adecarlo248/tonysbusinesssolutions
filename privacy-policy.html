Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2026

Plain English version: Tony's Business Solutions collects only the information needed to respond to inquiries, book demos, provide automation services, manage client workflows, and communicate with leads or customers when authorized by a client. We do not sell personal information.

1. Who we are

Tony's Business Solutions is an Ontario, Canada business that provides follow-up systems, booking automation, missed-call text back, voice reception, review requests, nurture workflows, website support, invoice follow-up, payment collection workflows, pipeline cleanup, custom automations, and related services for trades and local service businesses.

2. Information we collect

Depending on how you interact with us, we may collect:

  • Contact information, such as your name, business name, email address, phone number, and location.
  • Booking information, such as requested meeting time, appointment details, and notes you choose to provide.
  • Business information needed to scope or deliver services, such as services offered, business hours, service areas, lead sources, booking preferences, staff routing preferences, review links, website links, and workflow requirements.
  • Communications you send to us by email, phone, forms, booking tools, SMS, social media, or other channels.
  • Technical information from website visits, such as device/browser details, IP address, referral source, and basic usage data, where collected by hosting, analytics, security, or embedded third-party tools.
  • Client-authorized lead/customer information processed inside automation workflows, such as names, phone numbers, emails, appointment details, call/SMS history, review request status, and follow-up status.

We do not intentionally collect sensitive financial, medical, government identification, or unnecessary personal information through this website.

3. How we collect information

We may collect information when you:

  • Book a demo or appointment through our booking links.
  • Call, text, email, or message us.
  • Request a quote, demo, website build, automation build, or support.
  • Use a client workflow we have built or manage, such as missed-call text back, booking, reminders, voice reception, review requests, or follow-up sequences.
  • Visit our website or interact with links, embedded tools, social pages, or review links.

4. Why we use personal information

We use personal information for reasonable business purposes, including to:

  • Respond to inquiries and provide customer support.
  • Book demos, appointments, consultations, and service calls.
  • Scope, build, test, launch, maintain, and improve automation workflows.
  • Send appointment confirmations, reminders, follow-ups, review requests, and service-related messages.
  • Manage client accounts, invoices, reporting, troubleshooting, and support requests.
  • Improve our website, services, messaging, and customer experience.
  • Meet legal, security, accounting, dispute-resolution, and compliance obligations.

By providing your information, booking a demo, contacting us, or using a workflow operated for one of our clients, you consent to the collection, use, and disclosure of your information as described in this policy.

For commercial electronic messages, including marketing emails or SMS messages, we aim to comply with Canada's Anti-Spam Legislation (CASL). Where required, messages will identify the sender and include a way to unsubscribe or stop receiving non-essential commercial messages. You can also contact us directly at tony@tonysbusinesssolutions.ca.

Transactional or service-related messages, such as appointment confirmations, reminders, support replies, or workflow updates, may still be sent when reasonably necessary to provide the requested service.
