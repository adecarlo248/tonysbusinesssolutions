FAQ

Common Questions

Where does the data come from?

Directly from the FSRA (Financial Services Regulatory Authority) public registry — the official Ontario government database of all licensed and lapsed insurance agents. This is 100% public record data.

Is this legal to use for recruiting?

Yes. FSRA registration data is publicly available and intended to be accessible. Phone numbers are sourced from public directories (Canada411, 411.ca). Always follow CASL and PIPEDA guidelines when making contact.

How current is the data?

All lists are pulled fresh at time of order. Agents listed have licences that expired within the last 1–2 years — recent enough to be reachable and warm enough to remember why they got licensed.

What format will I receive the list in?

You'll receive a professional PDF report plus a CSV file you can import directly into any CRM — GHL, Salesforce, HubSpot, or a simple spreadsheet.

Can I get cities outside Central Ontario?

Absolutely. We can pull any Ontario city from the FSRA registry. Custom cities are $497 with 48-hour delivery. Book a call and tell us where you need to recruit.

What's your hit rate on phone numbers?

Our most recent batch returned direct phone numbers for over 80% of agents. The remaining contacts are still included — some may be reachable via LinkedIn or other channels.
